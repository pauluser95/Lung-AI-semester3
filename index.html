About Us

In the face of the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, health departments are leveraging cutting-edge technologies to enhance disease detection. Our commitment lies in deploying image processing artificial intelligence (AI) capable of swiftly and accurately identifying COVID-19 manifestations in medical imaging. Moreover, our AI extends its capabilities to detect pneumonia, a common complication of respiratory infections. By integrating these advanced tools, we aim to revolutionize diagnostic processes, contributing to a more proactive and effective public health response.


Our Goal :


  • Early Intervention: Detect COVID-19 and pneumonia early for prompt medical action.
  • Precision: Ensure high accuracy in identifying disease patterns for reliable diagnostics.
  • Streamlined Workflow: Integrate seamlessly into healthcare systems, optimizing resources and reducing delays.
